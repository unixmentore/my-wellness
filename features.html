White-label tele-counseling platform

Video and Audio Calls: The tele-counseling platform allows for video and audio calls, enabling individuals to have real-time counseling sessions with their therapists. This face-to-face interaction helps establish a stronger therapeutic connection and promotes effective communication.

Compliant with regulations

A reputable tele-counseling platform adheres to relevant healthcare regulations and privacy laws to ensure compliance and protect client data.

Treatment plan with goals

The patient dashboard may provide access to treatment plans, goals, and progress tracking. Patients can view their personalized treatment objectives, monitor their progress, and actively participate in their own mental health journey.

Outcome Tracking

Patients can track their mental health progress through the dashboard. This may include self-assessment tools, mood tracking features, or symptom monitoring, allowing patients to reflect on their well-being and share valuable insights with their therapist.

Remainders and notifications

Patients can receive reminders and notifications about upcoming appointments, therapy-related tasks, or new messages from their therapists. This helps patients stay engaged and informed throughout their therapy journey.

Assesment tools

The dashboard may include assessment tools that therapists can use to conduct evaluations, screen for symptoms, or gather information about clients' mental health. The results can be recorded and used to inform treatment decisions.

Treatment planning and goal setting

herapists can create personalized treatment plans, set goals, and track progress for each client. They can document treatment objectives, interventions, and milestones, making it easier to measure progress and adjust the treatment approach as needed.
