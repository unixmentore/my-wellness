Features

Empowering health saving lives

Appointment Management

Patients can view their upcoming appointments, schedule new sessions, or request appointment changes through their dashboard. This provides them with flexibility and control over their therapy schedule.

Outcome Tracking

Patients can track their mental health progress through the dashboard. This may include self-assessment tools, mood tracking features, or symptom monitoring, allowing patients to reflect on their well-being and share valuable insights with their therapist.

Self Help resources

The dashboard will access to self-help resources, educational materials, or exercises tailored to the patient's needs. Patients can explore these resources at their own pace, enhancing their understanding of mental health and supporting their therapy process.
